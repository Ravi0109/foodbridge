About FoodBridge

Building a sustainable future by connecting surplus food with those in need

Our Story

FoodBridge was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful mission: to eliminate food waste and fight hunger. Every year, millions of tonnes of perfectly good food are discarded, while countless people struggle to find their next meal. We asked ourselves — what if we could bridge that gap?

Our team of passionate individuals came together to build a digital platform that connects food donors with recipients quickly, efficiently, and transparently. By leveraging technology and community collaboration, FoodBridge aims to make food redistribution simple, sustainable, and impactful. Today, we would be proud to be part of a growing movement that will transform the way India manages food waste — one meal at a time.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and ensure no one goes hungry by connecting surplus food from donors with those in need through an efficient, transparent platform.

Our Vision

A world where food waste is eliminated, communities are fed, and sustainability is the norm. We envision zero food waste in every city.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Protecting our planet
  • Transparency: Building trust
  • Empathy: Caring for communities
  • Efficiency: Making impact at scale
