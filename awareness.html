Why Food Waste Matters

Global Crisis

1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted annually worldwide, while 690 million people face hunger.

Environmental Impact

25% of global freshwater and 28% of agricultural land produce food that is never eaten.

Climate Change

Food waste contributes to 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions — more than aviation industry!

Economic Loss

Global food waste costs the economy over $1 trillion annually, impacting development and livelihoods.
