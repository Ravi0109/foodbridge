Frequently Asked Questions

Can I donate leftover food from events?

Yes! Event leftover food that's packaged properly and within the expiry window is welcome. Ensure it's been stored at proper temperature.

Is there a cost to donate?

No! Donating through FoodBridge is completely free. We handle logistics at no cost to you.

How quickly will my food be picked up?

We typically pick up food within 2-4 hours of your listing. Urgent pickups can be arranged with our support team.

Do I get a tax receipt?

Yes! After verification, all donations are eligible for tax deduction. We provide certificates automatically.
