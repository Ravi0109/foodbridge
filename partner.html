Available Volunteer Roles

Food Collector

Pick up surplus food from donors and transport to collection centers

Time Commitment: 4-8 hours/week

Skills: Driving, physical activity

Distribution Assistant

Help distribute food to shelters, NGOs, and communities in need

Time Commitment: 3-6 hours/week

Skills: Organization, teamwork

Community Outreach

Connect with donors and recipients to expand our network and impact

Time Commitment: 5-10 hours/week

Skills: Communication, networking

Tech & Data Support

Help with website maintenance, app development, and data management

Time Commitment: Flexible

Skills: Programming, design, data analysis

Social Media & Content

Create content, manage social media, and tell our impact stories

Time Commitment: 5-10 hours/week

Skills: Photography, writing, social media

Data Analyst

Track and analyze impact metrics and generate reports

Time Commitment: 4-6 hours/week

Skills: Excel, analytics, reporting
