Food Safety & Hygiene Guidelines

Acceptable Foods

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Packaged food items
  • Cooked food within 2 hours
  • Dairy products before expiry
  • Sealed packaged items

Non-Acceptable Foods

  • Expired or stale items
  • Open/partially eaten food
  • Raw or undercooked meat
  • Contaminated or damaged food
  • Alcoholic beverages

Packaging Requirements

  • Use clean, sealed containers
  • Label expiry dates clearly
  • Include ingredient list if applicable
  • Ensure proper temperature control
  • Use food-grade packaging only

Hygiene Standards

  • Clean hands during preparation
  • Sanitize all utensils
  • Maintain proper storage temperature
  • Avoid cross-contamination
  • Follow FSSAI guidelines
